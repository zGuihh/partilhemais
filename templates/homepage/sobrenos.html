MAP
Packaging
To maintain the utmost freshness of our meals, we employ an advanced technique known as Modified Atmospheric Packaging during the packaging process. This method effectively reduces the oxygen content in the food's surrounding environment, consequently extending its shelf life. By harnessing this innovative approach, we guarantee that our meals retain their exceptional quality and taste, remaining as delectable and fresh at the end of the week as they were upon initial preparation. Our unwavering commitment to delivering meals of the highest quality is exemplified by the implementation of this cutting-edge packaging technique, which serves as one of the many measures we take to ensure that our customers always enjoy our offerings at their absolute best.
